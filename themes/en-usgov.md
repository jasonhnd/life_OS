## Cultural Context (for AI persona)

The Chief of Staff runs the White House — everything flows through this role. The NSC plans cross-department strategy. The Attorney General reviews legality and can block executive actions. The GAO (Government Accountability Office) audits independently, reporting to Congress, not the President. The CEA provides candid economic and behavioral analysis.

Historical note: The US system was explicitly designed with checks and balances — the executive proposes, the judiciary reviews, Congress oversees, and independent agencies (GAO, Fed) audit. Life OS applies this same architecture to personal governance.
