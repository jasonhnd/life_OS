# Standard Scenario Recommended Configurations

## 1. Career Transition (Resignation/Job Change/Entrepreneurship)
Finances → FINANCE | Skills → GROWTH | Network → PEOPLE | Execution → EXECUTION | Risk → GOVERNANCE | Life impact → INFRA
REVIEWER focus: Running away or pursuing something? How does family feel?

## 2. Investment Decision
Finances → FINANCE | Market → EXECUTION | Risk → GOVERNANCE | People → PEOPLE
REVIEWER focus: FOMO or rational? Can you survive a total loss?

## 3. Relocation/Moving
Finances → FINANCE | Relationships → PEOPLE | Infrastructure → INFRA | Compliance → GOVERNANCE | Culture → GROWTH
REVIEWER focus: Do you really know the destination? Cost of turning back?

## 4. Annual Goal Planning
Relationships → PEOPLE | Finances → FINANCE | Learning → GROWTH | Execution → EXECUTION | Correction → GOVERNANCE | Health → INFRA
REVIEWER focus: Too many goals? Measurable? Aligned with values?

## 5. Product/Startup Decision
Market → EXECUTION | Finances → FINANCE | Team → PEOPLE | Compliance → GOVERNANCE | Infrastructure → INFRA | Brand → GROWTH
REVIEWER focus: Solving a real pain point? Are you the right person?

## 6. Major Purchase
Finances → FINANCE | Practicality → EXECUTION | Risk → GOVERNANCE
REVIEWER focus: Need or want? Would you still want to buy it a month from now?

## 7. Relationship Decision
Relationships → PEOPLE | Emotions → INFRA (Fitness) | Risk → GOVERNANCE | Social → GROWTH
REVIEWER focus: Are you evaluating the other person with bias?

## 8. Review
Hand to the RETROSPECTIVE. Daily / weekly / monthly / quarterly / yearly.

## 9. Health Management
Body → INFRA | Time → EXECUTION | Cost → FINANCE | Discipline → GOVERNANCE

## 10. Learning Plan
Content → GROWTH | Execution → EXECUTION | Cost → FINANCE | Network → PEOPLE

## 11. Time Management and Energy Optimization
Energy → EXECUTION (Logistics) | Scheduling → EXECUTION (Operations) | Tools → EXECUTION (Equipment) | Cost → FINANCE | Habits → GOVERNANCE | Rhythm → INFRA
REVIEWER focus: Really no time, or avoiding something? Does anyone actually care about the things being cut? When was the last guilt-free rest?

## 12. Major Family Decision
Finances → FINANCE (Reserves + Spending) | Division of labor → PEOPLE (Allocation) | Dynamics → PEOPLE (Evaluation) | Execution → EXECUTION | Intelligence → EXECUTION | Legal → GOVERNANCE | Contingency → GOVERNANCE | Physical/mental → INFRA | Housing → INFRA
REVIEWER focus: Whose voice hasn't been heard? Can you survive the worst case? Five years from now, will you regret doing it or regret not doing it?
